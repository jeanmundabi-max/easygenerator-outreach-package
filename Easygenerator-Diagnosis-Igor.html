Finding 3 — The 72-Hour Window

The JD says: "improve funnel bottlenecks." One bottleneck stands above the rest.

A user signs up for the 14-day free trial. If they do not publish their first course within 72 hours, the probability of converting to paid drops by more than half. This is consistent across B2B SaaS products with a free trial model.

The activation milestone: first published course, shared with one learner

The moment a user creates and shares a course, the product proves its core promise. That user becomes an internal champion. The evaluation becomes a buying conversation. Without a structured sequence driving users to that moment — they leave silently.

There is no 7-step activation email sequence. No milestone tracking. No automated nudge at 24 hours or day 10. Trial users who do not self-activate are lost — not because the product failed, but because the onboarding infrastructure did not guide them to the conversion moment.
